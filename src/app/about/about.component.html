A great workplace is one built on trust. Trust drives sustainable engagement and engagement drives business performance.

Trust is the defining principle of great workplaces — created through management’s credibility, the respect with which employees feel they are treated, and the extent to which employees expect to be treated fairly. The degree of pride and levels of authentic connection and camaraderie employees feel with one are additional essential components.

The most common flexible hours arrangements are: Flexible hours or flexi-time schemes.

For example, an employee works from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. rather than from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. When employees arrive at different times in the morning, this is called staggered hours.
